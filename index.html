Resources Designed for Multilingual Classrooms

The Open-Source Modular ELA Curriculum (OMEC) is a shared instructional framework that provides stable, rigorous lessons teachers can rely on, adapt, and build upon‚Äîwithout starting from scratch.


Designed as a plug-and-play system for grades 9-12 ELA, OMEC reduces planning burden while maintaining instructional coherence, rigor, and accessibility for all learners, especially multilingual students.


Our core principle: Language fluency doesn't dictate cognitive ability. Multilingual Students engage with the same rigorous analytical thinking as their peers, supported by scaffolded language structures that make complex ideas accessible.

  • 50 Short Story Modules with complete teaching materials
  • Concept Modules for deeper understanding
  • Bilingual Resources including Multilingual Learner Packets with Spanish
  • Creative Projects with choice and flexibility
  • Analytical & Social-Emotional Learning integrated throughout
  • Ready-to-Use Resources including rubrics, lesson plans, and student packets
  • Teacher Autonomy built in with modular design

A Growing, Open Resource


OMEC is an evolving project committed to expanding access to high-quality instructional materials. New modules, grade bands, subjects, and language supports will continuously be added to the collection.


The goal is simple: education improves when resources are shared, not siloed. By making a rigorous, thoughtfully designed curriculum freely available, we aim to reduce barriers for teachers and students everywhere‚Äîsupporting better instruction and deeper learning across classrooms and communities.
