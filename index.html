ELA Curriculum for Grades 9-12 Multilingual Learners

The Open-Source Modular Curriculum (OMC) is a comprehensive, Lego-style plug-and-play ELA curriculum system designed specifically for multilingual learners in grades 9-12.


Our core principle: cognitive work should not equal language complexity. Students engage with the same rigorous analytical thinking as their peers, supported by simplified language structures that make complex ideas accessible.

  • 50 Short Story Modules with complete teaching materials
  • Bilingual Resources including Multilingual Learner Packets with Spanish
  • Creative Projects with choice and flexibility
  • Analytical & Social-Emotional Learning integrated throughout
  • Ready-to-Use Resources including rubrics, lesson plans, and student packets
  • Teacher Autonomy built in with modular design
